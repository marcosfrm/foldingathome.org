However you joined Folding@home, you're not on your own. The community runs an active forum, a Discord with hundreds of donors online, and a maintained FAQ. The links below are where to start.

FAQ

Installation, points, passkeys, troubleshooting — the short answers to the most common questions.

Forum

Long-form discussion with experienced donors and moderators. Best for anything that needs a back-and-forth.

Discord

Real-time chat with the F@h community. Quick questions, hardware help, and release-day discussion.

GitHub Issues

Client bugs: file at fah-client-bastet/issues; Web Control bugs at fah-web-client-bastet/issues. Search both before filing.

Other ways to reach us

For press inquiries, partnership proposals, or anything that doesn't fit the channels above, see the contact page for the project manager's address and other contacts.
